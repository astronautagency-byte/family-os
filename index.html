Meet FamOS

Families run better on FamOS.

FamOS is a private family coordination app for shared calendars, Google Calendar sync, meal planning, grocery lists, tasks, family chat, household planning, and AI-assisted organization.

Seeing this simplified page on a tablet? The full FamOS app may still be loading, or your browser may need a Safari/iPadOS update. You can still use the links above to sign in or create your family space once the full app loads.

Purpose of the app

FamOS gives invited household members one private place to coordinate schedules, responsibilities, meals, shopping, messages, and household planning.

Why FamOS uses Google Calendar

When a user chooses to connect Google Calendar, FamOS requests calendar access so selected Google Calendar events can appear in the shared family calendar and new family events can be added back to writable connected calendars.

Privacy and control

Household data is visible only to invited members of the same FamOS home. Users can disconnect calendar access from Settings.
